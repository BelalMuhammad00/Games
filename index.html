Details Game

Title : Ultimate Pirates

Category : Strategy

Platform : Web Browser

Status : Live

Ultimate Pirates is a browser-based strategy MMO published for both desktop and mobile browsers by Gameforge. Developed by Moonmana and Grafit — who are known for their contributions to Bethesda and CD Projekt Red games — Ultimate Pirates features both PvE and PvP that will appeal to the pirate lovers among us. Sail the oceans, explore Caribbean islands and plunder all the treasure you can. Team up with other players or fight them and take all their loot — not just their treasure, but weapons. In addition to fighting other players for their booty, players will also find themselves up against NPCs and monsters of the deep. Of course, the end-goal is to end up as an “ultimate pirate”, which can only be achieved by building a pirate empire complete with ports, shipyards, buildings, and more.
